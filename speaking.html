Dialogue 1:

Student 1: Hey, look at that! it's the fruit section
.
Student 2: Yes, let's go! I love fruits.

Student 1: Tell me, what are your favorite fruits?

Student 2: My favorite fruits are grapes, oranges, apples and strawberries
.
Student 1: Amazing! Those fruits are my favorite too!









Dialogue 1:

Student 1: Let's buy some bread to eat at home!
.
Student 1:What do you want to eat?

Student 2: I want to eat some muffins cookies and crumpets with coffee.

Student 1: It sounds delicious!
.
Student 2: Yes, but we cannot forget to croissants for mom, she loves them.

Student 1: Yes, you are right!
.







Dialogue 3:

Student 1: Mom, where can I buy some domestic appliances for our new home?
.
Student 2: We can buy them at the supermarket.

Student 1: Really?

Student 2: Yes, there we can find cookers, a washing machine and a muffin cake and other things.
.
Student 1: Incredible! I want to go now!

Student 2: Yes, let's go.
.
